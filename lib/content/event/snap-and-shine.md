## Overview

- **Team size:** Maximum 2 members per team
- **Entry fee:** ₹100 per participant
- Judge's decision is **final**

## Format

##### Round 1 — Themed Reel Submission

**Themes (Choose One):**

- 🌿 Nature
- 🚗 Vehicles
- ⚙️ Engineering Life

**Video Requirements:**

- Format: **Original 9:16 vertical cinematic video**
- Duration: **7–30 seconds**
- Quality: **High resolution** with a strong story vibe
- Watermark: Must include **your name in the right corner**
- No offensive language, vulgarity, or religious/personal privacy violations

**Submission Guidelines:**

- Upload reel on **Instagram**
- Use hashtag: **#snapandshineBVCOEK26**
- Caption must include: **College Name** (e.g. BVCOE, Kolhapur) & **full names of both participants**

##### Round 2 — On-the-Spot Challenge

- Topic given **on the spot**
- Time limit: **1 hour**
- Includes a **surprise challenge element**

##### Round 3 — Final Showdown

- Topic given **on the spot**
- Time limit: **45 minutes**
