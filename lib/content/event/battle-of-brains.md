## Rules

- **2 members per team** — no changes allowed after registration
- Registered teams only
- Report **15 minutes early**
- No mobiles or smart gadgets allowed
- Quiz Master's decision is **final**
- Misconduct will result in **disqualification**

## Format

##### Round 1 — Screening _(MCQ Round)_

##### Round 2 — Rapid Fire

##### Round 3 — Visual / Audio Round

##### Final — Buzzer Round

- Winner determined by **highest total score**
- Tie-breaker: **3 buzzer questions**
