description: CAD Master is a technical drafting competition that evaluates participants' proficiency in computer-aided design. From aptitude screening to hands-on architectural drawing, contestants will demonstrate their precision, planning skills, and command over Autodesk AutoCAD in designing a complete residential layout.
---

## Format

#### Round 1 — Aptitude Test _(Elimination)_

- Basic CAD Knowledge
- **30 MCQs | 30 Marks**
- Time: **30 Minutes**
- Offline Test
- Venue: **Digital Classroom (Civil Dept.)**
- Top scorers qualify for Round 2

#### Round 2 — Drawing Test

- Design a **Residential Building**
- Criteria (Plot Area, FSI, etc.) given **15 minutes before**
- Draw: **Floor Plan, Elevation & Section**
- Software: **Autodesk AutoCAD**
- Time: **60 Minutes**
