## Rules

1. Each team can have a **maximum of two participants**
2. Competition will be conducted in a **single round**
3. The reel must be an **original creation**
4. The video should **not exceed 60 seconds**
5. Please use **one medium of language** for the reels/video
   - Accepted languages: English, Hindi, Marathi
6. Decision made by the judges will be **final and binding**
7. No interference will be tolerated

## Registration Steps

1. Fill out the registration form online
2. Pay the entry fee
3. Await confirmation on your registered email
