## Rules

- Participation: **Individual or team** (max 2 students)
- Posters must be **original**, based on science, technology, or innovation
- **Standard size:** 18×24 inches (1.5×2 ft) — must include title, concept, and illustrations
- Use **safe and eco-friendly** materials & colours
- **Presentation time:** 3–5 minutes per participant/team
- Open to **1st and 2nd year** degree/diploma students only
- Judge's decision is **final**
